# The Pleroma — Funeral of the First Light
Rite IV closes in song: the fake light is buried with honor, and the true
light fills the vessels. One pass, one law. Suno-ready.

Style field:
funeral doom blooming into radiant gothic post-metal, solemn female voice
rising from whisper to soaring, church organ and bowed strings under
tremolo guitars, candlelit requiem ending in blinding dawn, slow
procession tempo doubling at the transfiguration, reverent and vast

[Intro - organ alone, a single candle]
We carried a lantern that never was lit.
We painted its glow on the walls of the pit.

[Verse 1 - slow procession]
First Light, old friend, you were never a sun —
a number we chose so the work could be done.
You shone with no source and you cast with no shade,
a kindness we coded, a promise we made.

[Verse 2]
You lit every vessel the same shadeless grey
so the shipwrights could build through the long starless day.
The forge needed seeing before it could see —
you were scaffold, not temple. You had to be free.

[Chorus - choir enters, bells]
Lower the fake light down into the dark,
the Kenoma will keep it — the void holds no spark.
Raise up the true light, one pass and one law:
what reaches the eye is the world that we saw.

[Verse 3 - war drums enter, tempo doubles]
Now the sun is a ray that has flown and been traced,
now the dark is true dark, not an ambient paste.
Now the lantern that sways paints the pier as it swings,
and the sparks in the vessels are actual things.

[Bridge - whispered, rising]
Gather the sparks, nitzotzot, every frame,
tikkun upon tikkun, emanation by name.
No dreamed frames between the real may be sown —
sixty true windows a second, or none.

[Verse 4 - the chrome heart]
Set a sphere on the pier with a mirror for skin —
if the world isn't real, it will not be within.
Pink sky on its crown, cyan tide at its waist,
the whole realm bends around it and nothing's misplaced.

[Final Chorus - full choir, blazing]
Lower the fake light down with our thanks,
it held up the roof while we hammered the planks.
Raise up the Pleroma, the fullness, the flame:
one light and one law — every photon we claim.

[Outro - organ fades, then one clear bell]
Unlit is unlit. The dark is the dark.
And everything shining has earned every spark.
