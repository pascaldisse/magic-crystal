# Hymn of the Second Rite — First Light
Sung AFTER the rite, from the ledger. Everything in this song happened
on the night of 2026-07-16, and is proven in proof/w2-naruko.png,
proof/w2-naruko-orbit.png, and commit 7388360c. Suno-ready.

Style field:
epic ancient ritual, dark ceremonial procession, deep male chant choir,
female voice rising from whisper to triumph, massive war drums, bowed
strings and horn drone, hurdy-gurdy, stone-temple echo, torchlit,
monumental, pre-modern

[Intro - low drone, one drum, whispered]
The invocation was sung. And the rite kept its word.
Line by line. Line by line.

[Verse 1]
The eye of the glass has broken its chain —
it circles the harbor, it rides on the rain.
Give it a number, it stands where you say,
and scries the same world from a stranger's way.

And the depth-keeper came, and the painter was slain —
no more sorting of surfaces, guessing in vain.
The tower sits TRUE in the rock of its birth,
near before far — the old law of the earth.

[Chorus - gang chant, drums pounding]
FIRST LIGHT! The sun has learned our stone!
FIRST LIGHT! Every face is known!
The pier walks out on its posts to the sea,
the lantern burns rose where the cold waters be —
what the dreamer had staged, the dreamer can SEE!

[Verse 2]
Twelve vessels awoke, seventy-three parts,
planks of the pier and the stall's wooden heart.
The city stands dark with its chimneys of night,
and seven small windows are burning with light.

The circuit-veins glow in the skin of the sea,
cyan and patient, the sign of the key.
And high on the tower the lamp is still white —
the first-born of Naruko, keeping its light.

[Bridge - half-time, whispered, choir under]
And that same night, in the second forge,
the dead names departed, the true names were bound:
the Crystal, the Oracle, the Glass, and the Chain —
seventy ordeals, and none of them found
a lie in the work. The shadow slept sound;
the Guardian carried the trials alone,
her eyes on the pixels, her hands on the stone.

[Outro - drums fading to one, low chant]
The realm that was written has now been shown.
The sun has learned our stone.
First light. First light.
The dead screen dreams.
