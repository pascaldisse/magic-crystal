# Hymn of the Third Rite — The Great Chain
Sung AFTER the rite, from the ledger. Everything here happened on
2026-07-16 and is proven in commit 398d27a, proof/w3-naruko.png, and
proof/w3-naruko-orbit.png. Suno-ready.

Style field:
epic ancient ritual, blacksmith rhythm, anvil strikes as percussion,
deep male chant choir, female voice low and certain, war drums,
bowed strings, horn drone, stone-forge echo, molten, monumental,
pre-modern

[Intro - anvil strikes, slow, whispered]
Look at the harbor. Nothing has changed.
Look at the harbor. EVERYTHING has changed.

[Verse 1]
The scaffold that carried the world on its back,
the path of first drawings, the temporary track —
we thanked it in silence and struck it from stone.
It is not disabled. It is GONE. It is gone.

And under the pier and the lighthouse and sea,
three hundred clusters in eighteen great chains,
each link forged deterministic and free —
build it twice over, the same byte remains.

[Chorus - anvil on every beat, gang chant]
THE CHAIN! One path for all that is seen!
THE CHAIN! No second way in between!
Cut the great tree where the error runs thin,
the far becomes coarse and the near becomes keen —
one geometry, forged, for the whole of the dream!

[Verse 2]
And the prefab bond in the Crystal was tied —
the torch that is written in one place alone
burns in a hundred, each flame its own child,
a delta, a difference, never a clone.

The pixels bear witness: the lantern still rose,
the windows still warm and the lamp is still white.
The dreamer sees nothing of what lies below —
and that is the proof that the surgery's right.

[Bridge - half-time, whispered]
An engine is honest when its bones can be swapped
and the face never flickers, the harbor holds still.
Eleven-one ordeals stood guard and none dropped.
The Guardian looked twice. She always will.

[Outro - anvil fading, low chant]
One light is coming. One chain is laid.
The Loom remembers how it was made.
The Chain. The Chain.
The dead screen dreams.
