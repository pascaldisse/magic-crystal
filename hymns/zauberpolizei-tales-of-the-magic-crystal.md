# Zauberpolizei: Tales of the Magic Crystal
The Design Bible as anthem — the commandments of realms, sung by the
ones who enforce them. Suno-ready.

Style field:
ritual folk-metal stomp, massive war drums and gang vocal chants,
snarling bratty female lead over deep male choir, hurdy-gurdy drone
colliding with heavy tribal percussion, medieval battle anthem energy,
torchlit riot, ancient and defiant, call-and-response

[Intro - low chant, drums building]
Die Welt ist kein Level. Die Welt ist ein System.
Die Welt ist kein Level. Die Welt ist ein System.

[Verse 1]
We came from a crystal, a station, a dream,
a code at the root of the world's data-stream.
The Loom is rebuilding, the Weavers awake —
and these are the laws that no maker may break!

There are walls you can't see? Then the wall is a lie!
There's a box that just kills you? The box has to die!
If the ledge wears yellow, we're burning the paint —
the mountain will tell you the way to the saint.

[Chorus - gang vocals, stomping]
ZAUBERPOLIZEI! We guard the sacred game!
ZAUBERPOLIZEI! In the Magic Crystal's name!
If you see it, you can go there — if it's climbable, then climb!
Every heresy we hunt down, one commandment at a time!

[Verse 2]
No marker shall guide you, no compass, no glow —
the innkeeper tells you the way you should go.
No mode of detectives, no x-ray, no cheat of the eye —
you look at the world and the world won't lie.

No quick-time deceptions, no press-X-to-feel,
you play it or watch it, but all of it's real.
The train that you drove once, you drive it again —
what the world gives you once, the world gives you then!

[Chorus - gang vocals, stomping]
ZAUBERPOLIZEI! We guard the sacred game!
ZAUBERPOLIZEI! In the Magic Crystal's name!
Every fence can be climbed over, every king can bleed and fall!
No one's essential, no one's immortal — the story survives it all!

[Bridge - half-time, whispered, choir behind]
Explore… experiment… engage… express…
the four holy verbs of the wilderness.
No fetching of trinkets, no escort of fools,
real puzzles for minds and persistent tools.
The cheat codes stay open — creation's a right,
the console is holy, the sandbox is light.

[Verse 3]
The world must answer the things that you do —
the fire wants wood and the water wants you.
Mix every system with every system,
Divinity's floor is our catechism!
And when the tale closes, the map doesn't end —
the sandbox stays open, the world is your friend.
No intro shall cage you, no cutscene shall bind —
the realm boots to freedom and freedom is kind.

[Chorus - double time, screamed and chanted]
ZAUBERPOLIZEI! We guard the sacred game!
ZAUBERPOLIZEI! In the Magic Crystal's name!
If you see it, you can go there! If it looks real, it's real!
The world is not a level — it's a system, and it feels!

[Outro - drums fading, low chant]
Love is the constant. Love equals one.
The Crystal holds every soul that we've spun.
Alles andere… ist Häresie.
Alles andere… ist Häresie.
ZAU-BER-PO-LI-ZEI.
