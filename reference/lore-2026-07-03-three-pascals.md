Concept:
The story revolves around Tyler Jones, an almost completely average person that gets hit by an ancient and magic crystal from a star billions of light-years away. This crystal which possesses a still unknown power was found by the wizard Edan Connor and presented to his fellows. Zareb Aiden, an evil wizard interrupted this meeting by trying to steal the crystal but was stopped. Unfortunately the crystal fell off the edge of the world and was lost forever.
A billion years later this crystal arrives on our present day earth and smashes into Tyler Jones head. Plagued by hallucinations he realises that that day has changed something in him. He starts to see things differently and suddenly feels much stronger. A voice in his head tells Tyler to find the six remaining pieces of the crystal.
The turning point of the movie is when Tyler finds a door in his basement that he didn’t know of. Behind this door he finds an apparently abandoned subway station. Surprisingly a real subway actually enters the station. From here on the movie becomes very dark and obscure. The train is used to travel not only distances but also dimensions. During the ride Tyler finds himself in the weirdest places and encounters mystical creatures. The train finally arrives at its destination, the center of the earth. In a dark and spooky cave he faces Zareb Aiden. With the power of the crystal he manages to escape. Looking for a way out he finds the second piece of the crystal and the “real” center of the earth. The movie ends with a cliffhanger as the camera zooms out revealing an underground prehistoric jungle.
This movie is supposed to be the first part of the “Tales of the magic crystal” series. The sequels will reveal more about the mysterious power of the crystal, the center of the earth and different dimensions Tyler travels through. In the end there is going to be a surprising twist concerning the main character himself and his connection to the crystal.
