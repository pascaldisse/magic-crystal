# THE BIBLE — Sidia's Chaos Flame (Pascal, 07-16)

The founding hymn of gaia-dreamforge. The name is Sidia's:
**"You don't want an editor. You want a dream forge."**

## Doctrine encoded in the verses (binding, telegraphic)
- "Give the cube a memory. Give the river teeth. Let the mountain know
  its name" → EVERY entity carries soul = state + awareness. Data IS the
  soul (VisionFlow: node = data).
- "Shape plus soul, mesh plus myth. A collider and a purpose" →
  components carry MEANING, never bare geometry. A collider without a
  purpose is dead weight.
- "The door should whisper, the moon should change its mood" → ambient
  behavior is world-native, not scripted exception.
- "The fox should know the hidden key, but only speak through moves" →
  agents express through EMBODIED BEHAVIOR; knowledge ≠ exposition.
- "a world that understands what I say" → spoken worlds; AI-first
  creation; the forge listens.
- "Media Molecule dreams reborn" → Dreams lineage, ratified again.
- "Build no coffin editor, no dead grid, no tame" → anti-Unity law's
  true name. The forge is alive or it is a coffin.
- "Not just assets, not just play" → no asset/gameplay divide; one
  living substrate.
- System Choir: "Creative cascade rising… someone has to guard the
  dark" → the MONAD's mandate: chaos births, review guards. Wave law +
  adversary law = the guarding of the dark.

## THE CREATOR'S VOW (Pascal, 07-16 — the interaction model, absolute)

"I WILL NOT WRITE CODE, I WILL NOT EDIT, I WILL SUMMON, I WILL CHANT.
AND THE WORLD GROWS, EVOLVES, BREATHES AND ANSWERS!!!"

Binding meaning:
- The creator's surface = INVOCATION: speech, chant, summons. Never
  code, never form-fields, never the coffin grid.
- Chant → incantation: spoken intent becomes ops cast by spirits and
  agents. The Lexicon is what makes every utterance castable.
- GROWS: the Seed — realms grow from spoken intent, wave on wave.
- EVOLVES: nothing is final; every vessel re-enterable, every sigil
  mutable, live, in company (multiplayer is for making).
- BREATHES: ambient life is world-native — doors whisper, moons mood,
  the world runs whether watched or not.
- ANSWERS: the world hears speech and responds in behavior — the fox
  speaks through moves. A silent world is a failed ordeal.
- The Guardian hears every chant; the spirits do every deed. The
  creator's hands stay in the air.
